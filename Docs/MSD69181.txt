Important instructions for implementation of the above:  
 
1. An interim coexistence period for receiving  broadcast from both  existing and new parameters  shall be 
provided by the Exchange, after which Exchange shall discontinue the existing broadcast parameters . 
 
2. Members are required to ensure that all necessary SOP / policy / procedures / mechanism are in place to 
switch to existing parameters if required in LIVE during this co -existence period.  
 
3. Members are required to note  there is no change in UDP application version  and are advised to take 
market data broadcast from single parameter at a time  i.e. either existing or new parameter. Refer to the 
annexure provided below for the parameters.  
 
4. Existing  transcodes (except 7201, 7202 & 7130)  and new transcodes  (i.e. 17201, 17202 & 17130)  will be 
available in new  broadcast  parameters . For details regarding new transcodes, kindly refer relevant NNF API 
document (link provided in point 6). 
 
5. Details of coexistence and migration plan for Market data Broadcast, NEAT and NNF users are as below:  
Functionality  Date

refresh)  July 19, 202 5 
Release of new NEAT version (7.8.2 ) with new broadcast market data 
parameters  (5 depth 1 second refresh)  July 19, 202 5 
Discontinuation of existing market data broadcast parameters for NNF 
users (5 depth 1 second refresh)  Shall be communicated 
separately  
 
6. For further details of implementation, members may refer the latest NNF API document (Version 9.43 ) 
detailing the encryption protocol is available on Exchange website under path:  
https://www.nseindia.com/trade/platform -services -neat -trading -system -protocols    
 
7. Details of the applicable NEAT version shall be communicated vide separate circular.
